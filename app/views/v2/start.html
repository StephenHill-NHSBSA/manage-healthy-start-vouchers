Manage Healthy Start vouchers

This service allows operational staff to manage the Healthy Start scheme.

People will qualify for the Healthy Start scheme if they are either:

  • at least 10 weeks pregnant
  • have at least 1 child under 4 years old

In addition, they must be receiving an eligible benefit.

Information:

Anyone pregnant and under 18 will also be eligible for the Healthy Start scheme, even if they do not receive any benefits.
