Manage Healthy Start Vouchers Version 1.0

The Healthy Start scheme allows eligible applicants to get free vouchers every week to spend on milk, plain fresh and frozen fruit and vegetables, and infant formula milk. They are also entitled to free vitamins.

The 'Manage' service allows operational staff to manage the scheme e.g. Answering queries, changing details, adding new applicants etc...

'Mini Discovery'

Designing a 'Manage' service

Version 2

Hypothesis A

We believe that providing a method for CCS agents to manage information about Healthy Start applicants will be a useful feature for all applicants who are not 'passported' by having an approved benefit as it will not only allow current users of the scheme to be managed, but also provide new applicants with an additional route of applying via the call centre. This will align with us supporting an assisted digital route.
